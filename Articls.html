Novel coronavirus


Most people infected with the emerging corona virus do not need special treatments in most cases, as their cases are often simple, but for people with the emerging corona virus who suffer from other health problems, the situation is different, including those with diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, in addition to People with chronic respiratory diseases as well as people with cancer.

Those with other health problems have severe disease if it appears, and therefore prevention of infection with this emerging virus is the best way to deal with it, as this virus is transmitted through droplets caused by coughing or sneezing after saliva or nasal droplets reach others

Symptoms of the emerging corona virus


Symptoms can appear on people infected with the emerging corona virus after exposure to the virus two days or even after fourteen days, and it is worth noting that the symptoms range in severity, they may be simple symptoms in some cases, while they are very severe in others, and it should be noted that the symptoms that have been observed in multiple infected with the emerging corona virus.Among the symptoms that may appear on those infected with the emerging corona virus are the following:• High body temperature and chills.

• Shortness of breath.

• coughing;

• diarrhea. • People may feel nauseous, and it may worsen and they may vomit.

• A feeling of tiredness and general exhaustion. • The patient may lose part of the sense of smell or sense of taste.

• The patient may feel muscle pain or pain in the body in general.

• Sore throat.

• Congestion or a runny nose can occur

Diagnosis of the emerging corona disease


Diagnosis is made using the polymerase chain reaction (PCR) test after taking a swab from a person suspected of being infected with or having contact with a person infected with the emerging coronavirus.
